Hosted SmarterMail

Do You Like Exchange Mail, But Hate The Cost and Management? Meet SmarterMail – Our Talented Exchange & SBS Replacement

SmarterMail Enterprise is our powerful, cloud hosted email collaboration service. It was designed to help you connect better with customers, employees and partners. SmarterMail can increase productivity and drive new revenue with the enterprise-class collaboration features most people associate with expensive, fussy and complicated email servers such as Microsoft Exchange or Small Business Server (SBS).

With full Outlook compatibility and Exchange equivalent features including email, contacts, calendars, tasks, instant messaging and notes; SmarterMail is a reliable, economical alternative to on-premises email servers. And because we host it for you in our resilient cloud network, we deliver 100% uptime with no software version update costs or support expenses for you to worry about. Ever.

And only SmarterMail includes a Document Management feature that allows you to easily send oversized files to recipients without worrying about attachment size limitations.

Instant Communications

A fully integrated XMPP (Jabber) based instant messaging system is available to instantly communicate with users within your hosted domain at NO EXTRA COST. No more emailing co-workers and waiting for replies, wondering if they are away from their desk. Instantly see co-worker availability and communicate with your colleagues in real-time! On the go? No problem! Stability / Uptime

In the unlikely event that something goes wrong, we offer multiple (US and EU based) backup mail systems for redundancy. This way you never miss an email! In the unlikely event that our primary mail server does go down, our backup mail servers will instantly start taking and holding all the incoming mail, once the primary server is back online all held email will be delivered. SmarterTools has developed one of the most stable, efficient, high-performance email server solutions available! Still not convinced? Let our publicly available live server stats do the talking!

Add Affordable Email Message Archiving Any Time!

SmarterMail provides a full read-only message archive that can be searched at the mailbox, domain and system level for all inbound and outbound email messages. And it complies with Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX), the Health Industry Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the new Federal Rules of Civil Procedure (FRCP) and other regulatory requirements. Many service providers charge over $8 a month per mailbox just for message archiving services alone. So our powerful, searchable message archive feature makes our SmarterMail service a true bargain starting at just $3 per mailbox. Whether you need to produce an entire range of email messages for e-Discovery in a legal matter, or just need to restore an accidentally deleted message to a user mailbox, our archiving interface will help you find what you need when you need it, simply and easily.
